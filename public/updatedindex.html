How it works

From shelf to receipt in under 3 minutes

Step 1

Scan

Open the web app on your phone. Point the camera at any product's QR code to add it to your cart instantly.

Step 2

Review & Pay

Review your cart and choose EcoCash, bank card, or cash. Online payments confirm in seconds; cash customers get a reference number for the cashier.

Step 3

Get Your Receipt

Download a digital receipt as a PNG image, with a full itemised breakdown, transaction reference, and store details.

What's inside

Three interfaces, one shared database

No Queue

Customers scan & pay at their own pace with their own phone — without waiting at a staffed till.

Real-time Sync

Firebase pushes every stock change, payment, and approval instantly to all three interfaces simultaneously.

Multi-Payment

EcoCash mobile money, bank card, or in-person cash — all three methods handled in one checkout flow.

Admin Control

Full product catalogue management, inventory tracking, QR code generation, and sales analytics in one place.
